# Riven Stat Recommender

## Overview

[Click here to jump to the tool!](#riven-stat-tool)

This tool lets you look up recommended Riven stat combinations for any* weapon. Two things worth keeping in mind before using this:

- This list may not be 100% up to date. Newer weapons, recent balance changes, or meta discoveries may slip undetecte.
- These combinations are meant to be guidelines to help give you a reference when rolling or buying Rivens. If you've found a stat combo that works well for your build, that's a perfectly valid roll.

---
## Reading the Cards

Each card represents one set of recommended options.

- **Solo Stat Boxes** are mandatory or high priority.
- **Stats connected with '/'s** are all decent and interchangeable. Ideally fill out your remaining stats with these. 
- **Acceptable negatives** are listed at the bottom of each card. These are not all inclusive but list a few safe stats
