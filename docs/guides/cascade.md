## Overview

Void Cascade is a high-intensity endless mission type that's exclusive to the Zariman Ten Zero (unlocked after the Angels of Zariman). In it, Tenno must continuously cleanse Exolizers of Void manifestations to prevent a cascading Void collapse. 

Cascade missions are very popular due to their rewards, quick enemy scaling, and ramping difficulty which makes them a great place to stress test builds or chase a challenge. Most endless mission types can take anywhere from 2 to 8 hours to reach the enemy level cap of 9999, but Cascades allow you to face level cap enemies after 107 Exolizers (around 1 to 1.5 hours).

> **Note:** When run on Steel Path and paired with an Omnia Void Fissure, Cascades lets you farm a variety of items, becoming an incredibly rewarding mission type.

## Rewards

Rewards vary depending on the variation of Void Cascade mission you start:

| Mission | Rewards |
|----------|---------|
| Standard | Zariman Arcanes |
| Steel Path | Zariman Arcanes, Steel Essence |
| Omnia Void Fissure | Prime Parts, Void Traces |
| Steel Path + Omnia Fissure | All of the above |

Zariman Arcanes include several valuable options that can be sold for platinum, and
the remainder can be converted into Vosfor through [Arcane Dissolution](dissolution.md).
